The Brutalist 2024

★★★★ Watched 27 Jan 2025 1

I knew beforehand that this movie would be in the "I would never enjoy this in any other setting besides a theater" category. I wish I had the setup/patience/attention to watch films like this at home but I know I do not -- so it was off to the theater for me.

And I loved it! The score, the pacing, the acting, the DRIP, the subtle (and not subtle) holocaust imagery, the long takes, the lighting was all top tier.…
